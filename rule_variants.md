2. First noun on odd pages  
3. First noun starting with vowel  
4. First noun after a comma  
5. First noun in dialogue  
6. First noun of every 3rd paragraph  
7. First noun after "the"  
8. First noun in chapter title  
9. First noun on page 7, 17, 27...  
10. First noun after "and"
